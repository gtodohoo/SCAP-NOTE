# SCAP Note — Getting Started Guide

Thank you for purchasing SCAP Note! This lightweight utility is designed to help you sketch, trace, and take notes directly over your workspace.
SCAP Note short for Screen Capture Noting, will assist you on your school studying and project documents review.
---

## 🛠️ How to Install and Launch

Because this application is a standalone utility distributed outside the official Microsoft Store or Apple App Store, your operating system's security filters might display a warning on your first launch. This is completely normal for independent software. Follow these simple steps to open your app:

### 🪟 For Windows Users (.exe)
1. Unzip the downloaded folder to a location of your choice (e.g., your Desktop or Documents folder).
2. Double-click `SCAP Note.exe` to launch the application.
3. **If a "Windows Protected Your PC" (SmartScreen) popup appears:**
   - Click the link that says "More Info".
   - Click the "Run Anyway" button that appears at the bottom.
   - The app will open safely, and Windows will remember this preference for future launches.

### 🍏 For macOS Users (.app)
1. Unzip the downloaded folder and drag `SCAP Note.app` into your Applications folder.
2. **Do not double-click it normally on the very first launch.** Instead, **Right-Click** (or hold Control and click) the application icon and select **Open** from the context menu.
3. A security dialog box will open asking if you are sure you want to open it. Click **Open**.
4. Alternative Method: If it blocks you entirely, open your Mac's System Settings > Privacy & Security, scroll down to the "Security" section, and click "Open Anyway" next to the Transparent Note notice.

## 🚀 Quick Start & Basic Controls

Once opened, Transparent Note will float directly on top of your existing open software (Photoshop, CAD, Web Browser, etc.).

* **Adjust Transparency:** Use the opacity slider on the app toolbar to dynamically change how clear or solid your note interface is.
* **Infinite Canvas Panning:** Hold down your Middle Mouse Button (Scroll Wheel) or use the hand tool shortcut to pan infinitely across your workspace drawing surface.
* **Window Pinning:** The application is configured to stay "Always on Top" automatically so it won't get hidden when you click the window underneath.

When you run the application for the first time, you will be prompted to enter your license key.
1. Copy the unique license key delivered to your receipt email by Gumroad.
2. Paste it into the activation field in the app and click Activate.
3. Your key will validate securely online and authorize your device. (Each personal license permits activation on up to 2 devices simultaneously).
